7.1 Holy Bible *Genesis 1:26-28:God set man free and gave authority over everything, but our ancestors misused them and were not capable of achieving a holy life, that's why God gave authority to Jesus Christ his son to give us salvation and the kingdom of Heaven, and through Jesus Christ(he is the way, truth, life and holy spirit) we can achieve Holy life and Kingdom of heaven, by following and believing the Jesus Christ through the holy spirit(by filling us with Holy Spirit). We can fill ourselves with the Holy Spirit by baptizing ourselves and by walking in the way of Jesus Christ. (references: Mathew 28:19-20, Romans 10:9-10, Acts:2:38, Acts 4:12, Isaiah 52:13-Isaiah 53:12, Malachi 3:1-3, Matthew 3:17, john 3:16-18, john 14:16, john 14:26,Isaiah 56:1-6).

Note: We Must believe God Almighty came as Jesus Christ (refer to my topic: Proofs that God Almighty Jehovah came as Jesus Christ.), (Holy Spirit is nothing but the spirit of the God Almighty).

Note: God does not want us to sin because our sins cause problems for us and other people.

*Jeremiah 7:1-6 and Jeremiah 7:22 - 23 God is seeking righteousness from us but not the holy sacrifices.

*Jeremiah 25:5-6 God wants us all to stop doing evil things and do good.

*Zechariah 7:1-10 God's concern is all about righteousness, justice, kindness, and peace and not about the sacrifices and offerings.

*Ezekiel 18:21-23 God's concern is all about righteousness, justice, kindness,and peace.
