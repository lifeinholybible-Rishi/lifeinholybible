Book of Know Truth

Know Truth is a collection of spiritual writings revealed by the grace of God and the guidance of the Holy Spirit, formed through prayer and self-study of the Holy Bible.

This work consists of 100 books, each dedicated to an essential spiritual topic meant to guide individuals and generations toward truth, righteousness, and the understanding of God.

These writings are not created by human wisdom alone, but are shaped through Scripture, reflection, and prayer, to glorify God and reveal His character, His creation, and His unconditional love.
